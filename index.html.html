About us


Ur :Device

Ur :Device is an American multinational technology company that specializes in consumer electronics, software and online services headquartered in Cupertino, California, United States.

Ur Device is the largest technology company by revenue (totaling US$365.8 billion in 2021) and as of June 2022, is the world's biggest company by market capitalization, the fourth-largest personal computer vendor by unit sales and second-largest mobile phone manufacturer. It is one of the Big Five American information technology companies, alongside Alphabet, Amazon, Meta, and Microsoft.

Ur Device was founded as Ur :Device Computer Company on April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne to develop and sell Wozniak's Ur Device I personal computer. It was incorporated by Jobs and Wozniak as Ur Device Computer, Inc. in 1977 and the company's next computer, the Ur Device, became a best seller and one of the first mass-produced microcomputers.
